About

Dathaglar Global Resources Limted is a private limited liability company incorporated in accordance with the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria in the year 2017. It was established to provide General Contract Services, Supplies for general goods and services, Information and Communication Technology (ICT) products and services to private and public organizations in Nigeria, thus making them competitive in the global space through cost minimization and output maximization.

About Us

Dathaglar Global Resources Limited offers a wide range of professional services aimed at meeting the diverse needs of our clients. With a focus on quality, innovation, and cost-effectiveness, we strive to deliver exceptional solutions that empower organizations to thrive in the global marketplace

  • Properties Management

    We excel in providing comprehensive property management services, ensuring the smooth operation and maintenance of real estate assets. From property inspections and tenant management to lease administration and facility maintenance, we prioritize the value and performance of your properties.

  • General Contractor Services

    As a trusted general contractor, we specialize in construction projects of varying sizes and complexities. Our expert team handles all aspects of the construction process, including project planning, procurement, project management, and quality control. We deliver exceptional results while adhering to strict timelines and budgets.

  • Logistics and Supply Chain Solutions

    Our logistics department offers efficient and reliable supply chain solutions. Whether it's transportation, warehousing, inventory management, or distribution, we streamline your logistics operations to enhance efficiency and reduce costs. Our goal is to optimize your supply chain and improve overall business performance.

  • Maritime Services:

    We provide comprehensive maritime services, catering to the unique requirements of the maritime industry. Our expertise covers vessel management, maritime consultancy, port operations, and maritime training. With a strong emphasis on safety and compliance, we ensure smooth maritime operations and facilitate growth in this sector.

  • Procurement Solutions

    Our procurement department offers strategic sourcing and procurement solutions to help businesses acquire goods and services efficiently. We leverage our extensive network of suppliers and negotiate favorable contracts to optimize costs and ensure timely delivery of high-quality products.

  • Farms and Agro-Allied Services

    We promote agriculture and agro-allied activities through our dedicated department. From farming and crop production to livestock management and agro-processing, we support sustainable agricultural practices and contribute to food security.
